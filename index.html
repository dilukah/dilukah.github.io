    Robot aided Tele-rehabilitation

    I developed a tele-robotic system which could be used for remote physiotherapy of patients in rural areas. It provides a haptic feeling between the patient and therapist. Furthermore it identifies gravity disturbance from the weight of the patient's arm while the exercises are performed and compensates for the gravity disturbance such that the therapist will feel only the abnormalities of the patient's arm. Gravity compensation also improves the overall system stability.
